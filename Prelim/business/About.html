ABOUT OUR COMPANY

       was organized and registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission on March 12, 2013 with principal office located at No. 103 M. Francisco St., Brgy. Lingunan, Valenzuela City, Metro Manila, Philippines.

Our purpose is to engage in the business of trading and importing of goods such as automotive parts, accessories, equipment and heavy duty trucks. And we are aiming to help our customers to invest in long term machineries and equipment.

      Our Company skilled employee firmly believes that our combined skills are the Best tools to earn the trust and confidence of our clients. We assured you of excellent after-sales support which is reliable and responsive to your needs.
