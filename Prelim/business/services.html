REPAIR

   A. Before fault maintenance is performed, it is advantageous to inspect and test to avoid wrong actions for maintenance. B. Inspect whether air pressure of steering wheel is enough, whether both sides are equal and whether wheels are worn. C. Inspect oil mass in oil tank is enough and whether is within the specified oil level. D. Inspect whether oil filter is clean and smooth E. Inspect whether flexible pipe is twisted, whether kinking is appropriate and is blocked. F. Inspect whether power-assisted steering oil pump belt slips. G. Inspect whether the following connections are obviously damaged or are not adjusted.

(1). Joystick and universal joint; (2). Steering arm, draw bar, draw bar joint and universal joint; (3). Loosening situation of each steering connection.

SERVICE

   Failure description 1. Diesel engine is difficult to start, and there’s big sound of “kaka” at the moment of engine starting. When the vehicle is started, rotating speed can only reach 1500r/min, and diesel enters into Limping Home state, and flash code lamp is on. 2. Read flash code 112- crankshaft rotation sensor is in failure, and after checking, it is found that crankshaft rotating speed sensor plug is loose. Failure is eliminated after reinstallation.

   Fault causes and remedies: Connector is not contacted well (it is possible that the sensor is forgotten to connect or the sensor malfunctions), so connect the sensor again (or replace with new sensor)
